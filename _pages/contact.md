## What to include in your message

To get a useful first reply, share:

1. **What you are working on.** A sentence or two about the company / problem.
2. **What you need.** Full-time hire? 6-week consulting engagement? Specific eval / fine-tuning / deployment problem?
3. **Timeline and budget range.** Helps me say yes or no quickly without a back-and-forth.

If it is not a fit on my side, I will say so honestly and try to point you to someone who can help.
