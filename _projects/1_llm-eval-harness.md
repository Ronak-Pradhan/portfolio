A reusable evaluation harness for fine-tuned LLMs. The opinion behind it: most fine-tuning teams spend too little time on evals and too much time on training. This harness reverses the ratio &mdash; you write a YAML spec for your dataset and metrics, point it at your model, and get back precision-by-slice tables, ablation comparisons, and CSV exports ready for review.
